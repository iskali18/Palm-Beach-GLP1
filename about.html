About PalmBeachGLP1.com

Purpose: This is an informational directory resource designed to help individuals in Palm Beach County explore local clinics offering GLP-1 weight loss programs.

What This Site Is

PalmBeachGLP1.com is a local directory that provides educational information about GLP-1 receptor agonist medications (such as semaglutide and tirzepatide) and connects residents with clinics in Palm Beach County offering medically supervised weight loss programs.

The information on this site is compiled from publicly available sources including FDA prescribing information, major medical centers (Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic), and federal health resources (NIH, CDC, CMS).

What This Site Is Not

This site does not:

  • Provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations
  • Prescribe or dispense medications
  • Offer telehealth consultations
  • Endorse or recommend specific clinics
  • Guarantee results from any treatment program
  • Replace consultation with a licensed healthcare provider

Clinic Listings

Clinic listings are compiled from public information sources. Inclusion in this directory does not constitute an endorsement or recommendation. Users should independently verify all provider credentials, licenses, and program details before making any healthcare decisions.

Clinics are not charged for basic directory listings. This site may evolve to include advertising or sponsored placements in the future, which will be clearly disclosed.

Content Updates

Medical information changes rapidly. While we strive to keep content current, users should verify all information with healthcare providers and official sources. Each page displays a "last updated" date to help assess content freshness.

If you notice outdated or incorrect information, please contact us so we can review and update as needed.
