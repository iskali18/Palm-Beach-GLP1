Medication Risks and Side Effects

GLP-1 receptor agonist medications, including semaglutide and tirzepatide, are prescription medications that carry risks and potential side effects. These medications are not appropriate for everyone. Serious side effects can occur, including but not limited to:

  • Thyroid tumors, including cancer
  • Pancreatitis (inflammation of the pancreas)
  • Gallbladder problems
  • Kidney problems
  • Serious allergic reactions
  • Changes in vision in patients with type 2 diabetes
  • Increased heart rate
  • Suicidal thoughts or behaviors
  • Gastrointestinal issues (nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, constipation)

This is not a complete list. Consult the FDA-approved prescribing information for complete details on risks, warnings, contraindications, and side effects. Discuss all risks with your healthcare provider.
