FAQ

What’s the difference between semaglutide and tirzepatide?

Semaglutide is a GLP-1 medication, while tirzepatide acts on two pathways (often described as GLP-1 and GIP). Providers may recommend one or the other depending on medical history, goals, and availability.

How much do GLP-1 programs typically cost?

Pricing varies by clinic and program structure. Some plans include clinical visits, labs, and coaching, while others focus mainly on medication management. Compare what’s included before deciding.

How do I choose a clinic?

Look for medically supervised programs, clear follow-up schedules, transparent pricing, and a plan that matches your preferences (in-person vs telehealth, coaching support, lab monitoring, and ongoing maintenance options).
